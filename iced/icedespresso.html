ICED ESPRESSO


About

If you drink coffee on a regular basis, you probably find yourself sipping on a specialty coffee drink made with a shot of espresso from time to time. Hot espresso drinks, like lattes and Americanos, are super popular among coffee enthusiasts, so it’s no surprise that you and so many others across the globe are drinking them often. But maybe you’re a fan of cold drinks and want to enjoy something over ice that has no foam. You could make a simple iced coffee, sure, but what if you need a drink that’s a bit stronger? Well, that’s when a good old iced espresso hits the spot! It’s exactly what it sounds like: espresso served over ice cubes. It’s just like cooling down with an iced coffee, but it’s got a slightly more powerful kick.


Recipe

Ingredients

  • Glass of ice
  • 3 shots of espresso
  • 2 tablespoons of heavy cream (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare your 3 shots of espresso.
  2. Pour espresso directly over your ice. Add more ice to your liking.
  3. Add milk and sugar to taste or serve as is!

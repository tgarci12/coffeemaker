MACCHIATO


About

Many drinks have been called by this name, but this (seemingly simple) traditional Italian recipe is the real deal. The word "macchiato" actually means marked.


Recipe

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground espresso
  • Cold milk

Instructions

  1. Brew one shot of fresh espresso with espresso machine.
  2. Steam milk to 160Â°, and hold the steam wand just below the surface to create foam.
  3. Pour milk into espresso to taste and spoon foam on top. It might take a few tries to get your personal ratio right!
